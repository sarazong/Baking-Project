Modified

December 30, 2025

Chocolate Souffles (amount: two 8oz ramekins)

Quantity
1. large eggs (50g each without shell) 2
2. chocolate (60 to 65 percent cacao) 75 g
3. unsalted butter 38 g
4. sugar for egg white 16 g
5. sea salt pinch
6. powder sugar 1 Tbsp

Step by Step

  1. Grease ramekins with butter and coat with sugar.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter over simmering water.
  3. Let the chocolate cool slight to warm and add egg yolks and salt.
  4. Whisk the egg white with a hand mixer and add sugar 1/3 at a time, until soft peaks.
  5. Whip the mixture in step 3 with a hand mixer.
  6. Fold in the meringue 1/3 at a time with a spatula.
  7. Divide the batter among the ramekins.
  8. Bake at 400F for about 15mins
  9. Dust with powdered sugar immediately after taking out.
